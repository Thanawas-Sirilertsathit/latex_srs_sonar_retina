\chapter*{Abstract}
\label{chap:abstract}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{\nameref{chap:abstract}}

For deaf, the impact of hearing loss is the disconnection from social environment.
This project introduces a mobile application that uses AI to restore the missing ability by visualizing sound in spatial context.
Rather than focusing on emergencies and safety, this app identifies and locates sound in daily life such as bird sound and baby crying.
By converting those sounds into 2D visual information, the application serves as an assessibility tool that transforms sound into a visible location and restoring a sense of hearing.
