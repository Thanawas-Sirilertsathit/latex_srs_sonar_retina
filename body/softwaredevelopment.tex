\section{Schedule}
\label{section:schedule}
\begin{figure}[ht]
    \centering
    \includegraphics[width=1\textwidth, height=8cm, keepaspectratio]{assets/timeline/timeline.png}
    \caption{Project Schedule.}
\end{figure}

The development of Sonar Retina follows a modular, priority-based timeline to ensure high precision and low latency in critical AI components.

Phase 1: SED Development (April 2026): The initial phase focuses on the Sound Event Detection (SED) infrastructure. 
This includes the implementation of the \textit{Audio Stream Dispatcher}, the \textit{Audio Processor} optimized for spectrogram extraction, and the core \textit{SED Service}. 
By completing this section first, the project allows for an extended period of model fine-tuning and latency optimization throughout the remainder of 2026.

Phase 2: SSL and Integration (January 2027): Following the stabilization of the SED service, development shifts to the Sound Source Localization (SSL) components. 
This involves implementing the \textit{Audio Processor} for phase-difference analysis and the \textit{SSL Service}. 
Concurrently, the \textit{Data Fusion Engine} and \textit{UI Controller} will begin development to manage the synchronization of spatial and semantic data.

Phase 3: Final Integration and Deployment (February and March 2027): The final development sprint in February 2027 focuses on refining the \textit{UI Controller} and \textit{Data Fusion Engine} to ensure seamless visualization of results on the Radar UI. 
The project concludes in March 2027 with final system deployment and comprehensive validation.
